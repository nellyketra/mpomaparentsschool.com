About Us

Welcome to Mpoma Parents School

Mpoma Parents School is a vibrant learning community that embraces diversity and inclusivity. Situated in Seeta, Mukono, we offer a comprehensive primary education program that caters to children from all walks of life. Our experienced educators are passionate about fostering a love for learning and guiding pupils to reach their full potential.
